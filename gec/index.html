Team Horizon at BHASHA Task 1: Multilingual IndicGEC with Transformer-based Grammatical Error Correction Models

Manav Dhamecha · Gaurav Damor · Sunil Choudhary · Pruthwik Mishra

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology, Surat

Abstract

We explore transformer-based multilingual models (mT5-small and IndicBART) for grammatical error correction across five Indic languages: Bangla, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam. To address limited annotated data, we introduce a linguistically informed synthetic error injection pipeline that generates realistic grammatical errors across multiple linguistic categories.

Method

Our approach combines multilingual transformer fine-tuning with synthetic data augmentation. The pipeline introduces errors such as spelling, grammatical agreement, missing tokens, punctuation, semantic substitutions, and structural inconsistencies.
